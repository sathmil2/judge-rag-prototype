750 ILCS 60/214 - Order of protection remedies

For prototype and educational use only. Replace this sample with official text from the Illinois Compiled Statutes or court rule source before relying on it.

A court may grant remedies in an order of protection when supported by the petition, evidence, and applicable law. Remedies may include protection from abuse, stay-away provisions, exclusive possession of a residence, counseling, and other relief authorized by law.
